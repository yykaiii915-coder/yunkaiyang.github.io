About Me

I am Yunkai Yang, a Research Assistant at Shenzhen University supervised by Prof. Zhenhong He. In September 2026, I will move to Hong Kong to pursue my MSc in Neuroscience at the City University of Hong Kong.

I obtained my B.S. in Applied Psychology from Henan University in 2025. My research interests lie at the intersection of Social Neuroscience and Computational Neuroscience. I have extensive experience in multimodal neuroimaging techniques, including fMRI, fNIRS, EEG, and MEG, and I apply advanced computational methods such as MVPA, HMM, and deep learning (CNN, VAE) to understand the neural mechanisms of social behavior and emotional regulation.
